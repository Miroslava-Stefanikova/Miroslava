Christmas will come when:

We let the snow come down and wash away our tears.
We let the snow shatter the walls for a new sun.
Then we will see a light in the sky and
we will be touch by an angel with love who
will bring us encouraging challenges.

Therefore

Do not forget that: "The biggest poem of wishes and desires
has been written million years ago. Everyone of us has to find it alone".

Glamorous and Marvellous Christmas.
